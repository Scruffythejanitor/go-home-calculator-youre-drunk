HOW TO PLAY:

GO HOME CALCULATOR, YOU'RE DRUNK

Your goal is to match your number to the... number to match. How do you do that you ask? Why, that's a swell question! See those big-ass buttons with the rando junk on them? Those buttons contain secret, hidden numbers. Clicking them will add whatever that hidden number is to your total. So PAY ATTENTION! Mental math is your friend.
Well that's about it...
OH! Just one more thing! That reset button does... you guessed it! It sends me a personal message that you have given up!
Have o-so-much fun!
